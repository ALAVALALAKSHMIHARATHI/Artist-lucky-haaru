Types of arts

painting

Painting is a form of artistic expression that involves applying pigments, such as paint, ink, or dye, onto a surface to create a visual composition. It encompasses a wide range of techniques, styles, and mediums. Common surfaces for painting include canvas, paper, wood, walls, or glass, and popular mediums include watercolor, acrylic, oil paints, and gouache.

sketch

A sketch is a quick, freehand drawing or draft created to capture the basic form, proportions, and essence of a subject. It often serves as a preliminary study or outline for a more detailed artwork, design, or idea. Sketches are typically made using simple tools such as pencils, charcoal, ink, or pastels on paper, focusing on simplicity and spontaneity rather than refined details.

paper cutting

Paper cutting art is a creative and intricate craft that involves cutting paper into decorative designs, patterns, or images. This art form has been practiced for centuries across different cultures, including Chinese paper cutting (Jianzhi), Japanese Kirigami, and Polish Wycinanki, each with its own unique styles and traditions.
